# Third-Party Assets

PufferDesk keeps bundled media original, licensed for redistribution, or otherwise release-safe.

Selected app, document, and virtual folder SVG glyphs in
assets/media/shared/icons/theme/ are based on Tabler Icons v3.44.0 under the
MIT License. The surrounding app icon wrappers, gradients, colors, and theme
styling are PufferDesk assets. See THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.txt for the full
license notice.

Built-in wallpapers are CSS-defined gradients declared in the theme registry. No built-in wallpaper image files are currently bundled.

If future releases include third-party media, document each asset here with its source, license, file location, and redistribution notes.
