# Third-Party Assets

Admin OS Mode avoids bundled Apple-owned, Microsoft-owned, Canonical-owned, and other third-party platform artwork, app logos, wallpapers, and font files.

## Aqua Glass Icon Pack

Location: `assets/media/themes/macos/default/icons/`

The optional Aqua Glass app, folder, dock, and widget icons use original rounded tile wrappers with glyphs derived from Lucide icons.

Source: https://github.com/lucide-icons/lucide

License: ISC for Lucide Icons and Contributors. Lucide also documents that some icons are Feather-derived under MIT.

Lucide source glyphs used:

- `layout-dashboard.svg`
- `file-text.svg`
- `files.svg`

- `message-circle.svg`
- `palette.svg`

- `clock.svg`
