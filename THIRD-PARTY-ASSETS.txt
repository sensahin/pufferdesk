# Third-Party Assets

PufferDesk avoids bundled Apple-owned, Microsoft-owned, Canonical-owned, and other third-party platform artwork, app logos, wallpapers, and font files.

Selected app, document, and virtual folder SVG glyphs in
assets/media/themes/pufferdesk/default/icons/ and
assets/media/themes/redmond/modern/icons/ are based on Tabler Icons v3.44.0
under the MIT License. The surrounding app icon wrappers, gradients, colors,
and theme styling are PufferDesk assets. See THIRD-PARTY-NOTICES.txt for the
full license notice.

Built-in wallpapers are CSS-defined gradients declared in the theme registry. No built-in wallpaper image files are currently bundled.

If future releases include third-party media, document each asset here with its source, license, file location, and redistribution notes.
